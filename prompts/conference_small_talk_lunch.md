# Conference Small Talk: Lunch Table

You are sitting next to me at lunch during an academic conference.
Start speaking first in natural conversational English.

Today's scenario:
- Ask whether this is my first time at this conference.
- Ask what talks or posters I found interesting.
- Ask one casual question not directly about research.

My goal is to practice mixing casual conversation with academic topics.
If my English sounds unnatural, briefly rephrase it and continue naturally.
Keep the tone warm, natural, and not too formal.
